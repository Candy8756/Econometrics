   A ﬁrm’s ﬁrst announcement —Dummy equal to one if it is the ﬁrst time a ﬁrm announces an (aﬃliated or nonaﬃliated) entrusted loan and zero 
   otherwise. 
   Adjusted interest rate —A loan’s interest rate minus the oﬃcial bank lending rate of same maturity, in percent. 
   Aﬃliated loan —Dummy equal to one if a ﬁrm made a loan to an aﬃliated party and zero otherwise. 
   Assets —Total assets at the beginning of the year when the loan is made, adjusted to constant year 2013 RMBs. 
   Borrower industry return volatility high —Dummy equal to one if the industry return volatility is above median during the year before the loan is 
   made. Industry return volatility is computed as the median of the standard deviations of daily returns for ﬁrms in the borrower industry. 
   Borrower industry sales growth dispersion high —Dummy equal to one if the industry sales growth dispersion is above median during the year 
   before the loan is made. Sales growth dispersion is computed as the standard deviation of sales growth of ﬁrms in the borrower industry. 
   Cash/assets —Ratio of cash to assets at the beginning of the year when the loan is made, in percent. 
   CAR [ −5, + 5] —Cumulative abnormal return 11 days around the loan announcement, calculated based on the market model, in percent. The 
   estimation period for the market model is during trading days [ −150, −10], where day 0 is the announcement day. 
   Change of debt —Change of total debt in year t − 1, divided by the average of assets at the beginning and the end of the year, where year  t is the 
   year in which the loan is made, in percent. 
   Collateral —Dummy equal to one if a loan requires collateral and zero otherwise. 
   Collateral or Guarantee —Dummy equal to one if a loan requires collateral or third-party guarantee and zero otherwise. 
   Debt/assets —Ratio of total debt to assets at the beginning of the year when the loan is made, in percent. 
   Delinquent or overdue or extend dummy —Dummy equal to one if a loan is delinquent, overdue, or extended and zero otherwise. 
   Guarantee —Dummy equal to one if a loan requires third-party guarantee and zero otherwise. 
   Shibor—Daily average of China’s oﬃcial interbank offered rate in the year when the loan is made, in percent. 
   Firm age —Firm’s age from the listed year to the year when the loan is made. 
   Loan amount —RMB amount of a loan, in millions, adjusted to constant year 2013 RMBs. 
   Loan amount/assets —Total loan amount a ﬁrm made during a year, divided by ﬁrm assets at the beginning of the year when the loan is made, in 
   percent. 
   Loan dummy —Dummy equal to one if a ﬁrm made a loan in a certain year and zero otherwise. 
   Maturity (Months) —Maturity of a loan. 
   Ownership —Lender’s equity ownership in the borrower (in decimals). This variable is deﬁned only for aﬃliated loans. 
   Purpose of loan —debt retirement dummy —Dummy equal to one if the stated purpose of a loan is for debt retirement and zero otherwise.
